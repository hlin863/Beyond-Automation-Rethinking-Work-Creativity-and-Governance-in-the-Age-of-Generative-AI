Method integration: Level 1.5 autonomy

What Level 1.5 autonomy means

An intermediate autonomy stage: AI proposes/plans/validates, while humans retain final evaluative authority.

Why it matters

Makes accountability and auditability ‚Äúlegible‚Äù by formalising the human‚ÄìAI loop (accept/revise with constraints).

Inclusive AI Governance Framework (5 dimensions)
Governance Regulatory, ethical, and organisational structures aligned with democratic values and accountability.
Skills development Ensure workers can thrive in hybrid human‚ÄìAI workflows (beyond narrow technical upskilling).
Creativity preservation Safeguard autonomy and originality under alignment constraints and restrictive content policies.
Model design Transparency, contextual awareness, proportional safety; prevent echo chambers and sycophantic drift.
Economic security Integrate UBI as a stabilising mechanism inside a broader socio-technical system.
